Collinsville, IL

Collinsville is home to the Brooks Catsup Bottle Water Tower. They have an annual Italian Fest in September. Here I lived with my mother and both grandparents growing up.

Angel's Camp, CA

Angel's Camp is known for the annual Jumping Frog Jubilee. It was a Gold Rush town and Mark Twain wrote a book about the Jumping Frog Contest. Here I lived with my aunt Terri for a few weeks one summer.

Caseyville, IL

Caseyville started as a coal mining town. I lived here with some roommates.

Santa Fe, NM

Santa Fe is the oldest state capital in the USA and is a Spanish town from the 1600's. It is a beautiful mountain town with adobe style archetecture. They have an annual Fiesta where they burn a large puppet called Zozobra. Here I lived with my aunt Sue and uncle Bill.

Collinsville, IL

I moved back to Collinsville. I lived with my grandma Fern and then met my husband, Lonnie, and we moved into a little cottage and got married shortly after.

Fairview Heights, IL

I moved into a home and we started our family. I have a lovely daughter named Payton & a brilliant son named Donavin.
